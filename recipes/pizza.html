Pizza Recipes

Description:

This whole wheat pizza dough recipe bakes up into an amazing crust

that's crisp on the outside and soft and chewy on the inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
